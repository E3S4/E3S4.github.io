About FOE

FOE (Fusion of Effects) is my dream project it's an editing software designed to bring motion, visuals, and creativity together. FOE is my hobby project towards building a tool where code and art fuse perfectly. The reason for which im coding foe is just to get toe-to-toe alternative for After Effects.
